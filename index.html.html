How to play

  1. Add 2–4 players. Each picks a pawn color.
  2. On your turn, roll the dice and move that many tiles forward.
  3. The tile color tells you the card category. A card appears — answer it.
  4. Correct: stay on the tile and earn 1 point. Wrong: slide back 1 tile.
  5. First to land on the FINISH tile wins (overshoots bounce back).
  6. Some tiles are special — read what they say.
